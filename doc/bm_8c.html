BM algorithm function.

BM algorithm to find the match substring.

There are two rules in BM algorithm.

rule 1: bad charactor.

compare begin with last charactor: ! abceaebabcde abcd

'e' is a bad charactor, 'e' is not in 'abcd', move length 4('abcd'): ! abceaebabcde abcd

now 'a' is a bad charactor, 'a' in 'abcd', move to make 'a' align with 'a': 

      !

abceaebabcde abcd

rule 2: good suffix.

compare begin with last charactor: ! abceaabccabef aabccab

step to left: 'ab' is good suffix, 'a', 'c' not match: *!! abceaabccabef aabccab

move length 4 to make 'ab' align with 'ab': 

     !!

abceaabccabef aabccab
